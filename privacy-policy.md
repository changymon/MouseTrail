# Mouse Trail — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 16 July 2026_

Mouse Trail does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- The app has no network access of any kind.
- Mouse position is read only to draw the trail on your screen, in the
  moment, and is never recorded or stored.
- Your preferences (trail style, color, thickness, length) are saved
  locally on your Mac and never leave your device.
- There are no analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no third-party
  services.
